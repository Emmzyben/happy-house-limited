Professional After Party Cleaning

The Happy House Cleaning Services won’t leave a corner untouched! Our team starts by collecting and removing all plastic glasses, bottles, dishes/plates and other rubbish from around your home. They will then focus on the living room, cleaning and dusting all hard surface areas, mirrors and glass surfaces. Finally, they will either vacuum or mop it, depending on its texture.

Areas In Your Home We Pay Extra Attention:

  • Kitchen – the focal point of many parties! We start by removing rubbish and washing up dirty glasses, dishes, and plates. Then we scrub and shine the sink and taps before moving on to appliances, cupboards, and surfaces. We finish by vacuuming or mopping the floor.
  • Bathroom – we clean all surfaces, disinfecting and scrubbing the toilet, vanity unit, sink, and shower, then either vacuum or mop the floor.
  • Common areas – our team will remove marks on walls, clean mirrors or other glass surfaces, and dust. Again, they will finish by vacuuming or mopping the floor.
