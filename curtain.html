How Do Our Curtain Cleaners Work?

Your home draperies and curtains will be cleaned on-site by our cleaning teams, using the steam cleaning method. This is by far the most widely used method, and the most effective as well. Cleaning solution is sprayed on the fabrics, along with heated water to remove ingrained dirt and any stubborn stains. Then an industrial power vacuum removes the water along with any soil. Your curtains are left slightly damp, and should dry in a few hours.
