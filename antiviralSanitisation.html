Antiviral Sanitisation Service in London of your property and office

The Happy House specialists will use professional equipment and will wipe all washable surfaces down in order to eradicate and disinfect from all the harmful germs. Our broad spectrum and certified disinfectant is perfect for disease control or precautionary measure in any property or business, in order to ensure and maintain hygienic conditions. Our antiviral sanitisation service can be requested separately or combined with any of our other services.

BS EN 1276 is the European standard for the bactericidal activity of chemical disinfectants as proof of effective infection control against harmful micro-organisms such as MRSA, Salmonella, E.Coli, Flu Virus (H1N1) HIV(AIDS), T.B, SARS, Hepatitis, Influenza A. In order to pass this standard, products have been tested by an independent laboratory and must prove a bacteria kill rate of 99.999% within 5 minutes.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Can I be at the property during the process of sanitisation?

A: The sanitisation will take 1-3 hours, depending on the size of the property. It is recommended for the occupants to leave the property for around 2 hours after the procedure. Our treatment is safe and need only some ventilation upon completion.

Q: Do I need to prepare the property before the procedure?

A: There is no need of special preparation, however we suggest any small objects or valuable items to be put aside, so the technician will have unobstructed access to all the surfaces.

Q: Is the treatment safe for the property?

А: Yes, we use certified detergents, which are safe for any surface or material. They sanitise frequently touched items such as door handles, tables, banisters etc. and is suitable for damp mopping floors. Independently proven to kill the HIV, Hepatitis C and H1N1 Influenza viruses.

Q: Who will provide the service?

A: We will send cleaning operatives with the correct personal protective equipment with vast experience in cleaning and sanitisation.

Q: Can I combine the treatment with a different service?

A: Yes, you can! For even better results, we would recommend any of our cleaning offers to be performed prior to the disinfection, so you will have neat and sanitised property. We also provide preferential prices if you decide to combine services.
